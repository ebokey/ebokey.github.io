Hello, my name is Eric Bokenfohr. I recently completed my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering at UBC, specializing in mechatronics. I am passionate about all things engineering and the development of technology for a better world. That includes sustainable engineering, which I have a lot of experience with from my internships and UBC Supermileage. The impact my decisions make on energy consumption and the environment is an important consideration all of my engineering work.

My particular areas of specialty are controls and dynamics, modelling, and electromechanical systems. I also have a lot of experience with problem solving, and working in team environments of all shapes and sizes.

Outside of engineering I enjoy spending time playing hockey, hiking, climbing, and baking.
