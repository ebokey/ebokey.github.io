Hello there, my name is Eric Bokenfohr. I'm a mechanical engineering student at the University of British Columbia, specializing in mechatronics. I am also the team captain of the UBC Supermileage Team, and a teaching assistant for UBC's department of Mechanical Engineering. If I'm not working on-campus, you might find me baking at home, playing hockey, or rock climbing.

As captain of the UBC Supermileage Team, I am very passionate about sustainable engineering and have a lot of experience with raising awareness for sustainable transportation through my work on the team. The impact my design choices make on energy consumption and the environment is an important consideration in my design team work, and something I would like to bring into other types of engineering work.

I'm also very interested in mechatronics systems, particularly automobiles and robots.
